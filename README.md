# Healthcare Operations & Financial Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze hospital performance, patient demographics, and financial revenue streams using clinical data.

##  Project Overview
This project provides actionable insights for hospital management to optimize patient care and improve financial workflows. It is divided into two major analytics focus areas:
1. **Clinical Operations:** Tracking patient volume, common medical conditions, and doctor performance.
2. **Financial Analytics:** Analyzing revenue trends, billing distributions by insurance providers, and treatment costs.

##  Tech Stack & Skills Used
* **SQL:** Data extraction, filtering, and creating custom age groups.
* **Power BI Desktop:** Data modeling, DAX formulas (Calculated Columns), and interactive visualization design.
* **Data Visualizations:** Matrix tables, Line charts, Donut charts, and custom Slicers.

##  Key Business Insights

* **Financial Revenue Trends:** The hospital experienced peak billing periods during mid-2020 and early 2023, reaching up to $0.89M and $0.86M respectively.
* **Top Revenue Drivers:** Patients admitted for **Asthma** under **Elective Admissions** generated the highest overall billing amount ($18.50M).
* **High-Cost Treatments:** **Hypertension** and **Diabetes** carry the highest average treatment cost per patient (~$26.8K), signaling high resource utilization.
* **Admission Breakdown:** Revenue generation is balanced almost equally between Emergency (32.4%) and Elective (36.2%) admissions, indicating a healthy mix of urgent and routine clinical influx.

##  Dashboard Features
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Filter data instantly by Gender, Blood Type, Age Group, and Insurance Provider.
* **Cross-Filtering:** Selecting a specific category dynamically updates all KPI cards and charts across the page.
